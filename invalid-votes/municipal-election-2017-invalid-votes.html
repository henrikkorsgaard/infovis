Municipal Election 2017 Invalid Votes

Narrative

It's election time and soon we will fancy visualisations of projections and later results. We get to see simple bar visualisations showing who is leading and how the votes are distributed between the main two political blocks.


Another common visualisation is to show the results on a map indicating which block won which municipality.

Some of the visualisations will even allow us to explore the votes for each municipality, making it easier to explore the results in where we live.

But there is a group of votes that we rarely look at. At every election a few of the votes are categorised as invalid. These are votes that represent citizens moving to their local school, elder centre, city hall to cast their vote, but for various reasons the votes end up not counting.
